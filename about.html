Who am I?

I am a 22-year-old passionate about technology, computers, and LEGO since childhood. From a young age, I have always enjoyed assembling and disassembling things, whether it be LEGO sets, models, or technological devices. This passion led me to discover a similarity with programming, where creating and modifying blocks is essential to making everything work.

Another of my great passions is photography, which serves as a way to disconnect from programming. However, it remains closely related, as I thoroughly enjoy editing and creating photomontages. This often leads me to spend hours on the computer, composing unique pieces.

I love constantly evolving and exploring new technologies. For example, I enjoy diving into forums like Reddit to learn about the latest features of mobile devices or discovering new software for 3D editing. I am always looking to expand my knowledge and stay up to date.
